Our Story

Founded in 2024, MegaHTML Pro started with a simple mission: to make web development accessible to everyone. We believe that beautiful, responsive websites shouldn't require complex frameworks or expensive tools.

What began as a small side project has grown into a comprehensive template system used by thousands of developers worldwide. Our team is spread across the globe, united by a passion for clean code and great design.
